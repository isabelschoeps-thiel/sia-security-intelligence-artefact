## Abstract
This repository documents the forensic and legal analysis titled *SIA Security Intelligence Artefact*, confirming authorship, digital origin, and provenance through blockchain and AI automation systems.

The dataset includes forensic documentation, certificates, and SHA-256 hash verifications.  
Access to raw materials is restricted to authorized legal and scientific institutions.

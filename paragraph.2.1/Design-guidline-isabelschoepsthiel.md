# Premium Store Landing Page Design Guidelines

[design_guidelines](https://www.notion.so/design_guidelines-27cc29c50f7d81fa99decc61f1a22351?pvs=21)

## Design Approach

**Reference-Based Approach** - Drawing inspiration from premium e-commerce sites like luxury fashion retailers (Net-a-Porter, Mr Porter) and artisan marketplaces (Etsy’s premium segment). The design emphasizes craftsmanship, heritage, and premium quality through sophisticated visual presentation.

## Core Design Elements

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep charcoal: 220 15% 15% (primary text, headers)
- Warm cream: 45 25% 92% (backgrounds, cards)
- Rich brown: 25 40% 25% (accents, premium feel)

**Dark Mode:**
- Charcoal black: 220 20% 8% (backgrounds)
- Soft white: 45 15% 88% (text)
- Warm gold: 35 65% 65% (accents)

**Gradients:** Subtle warm-to-cool gradients on hero section (cream to light brown) and section dividers.

### Typography

- **Primary:** Playfair Display (serif, elegant headers)
- **Secondary:** Inter (clean body text, product details)
- **Accent:** Crimson Text (brand story, craftsmanship details)

### Layout System

Tailwind spacing units: 4, 8, 12, 16 for consistent rhythm and premium feel with generous whitespace.

### Component Library

- **Hero Section:** Full-viewport with overlay text and CTA buttons
- **Gallery Grid:** Masonry-style product display with hover details
- **Product Cards:** Clean borders, subtle shadows, premium imagery
- **Brand Story Cards:** Image-text combinations with elegant typography
- **Navigation:** Minimal, sticky header with elegant typography

## Page Structure (4 Sections)

### 1. Hero Section

Full-viewport showcase of signature hat collection with elegant overlay text, brand tagline, and primary CTA. Features premium lifestyle imagery.

### 2. Featured Collection Gallery

Interactive grid displaying 12-16 premium hats with filtering by style, material, and price. Hover states reveal quick details and “View Details” buttons.

### 3. Brand Heritage & Craftsmanship

Split-layout section combining brand story with sourcing information. Includes timeline of craftsmanship process and sustainability commitments.

### 4. Premium Experience

Final section highlighting customer service, sizing guides, and contact information with subtle call-to-action for browsing full collection.

## Images

- **Large Hero Image:** Yes - Premium lifestyle shot of person wearing signature hat in elegant setting
- **Product Gallery:** 12-16 high-quality product shots on neutral backgrounds
- **Craftsmanship Images:** 3-4 images showing hat-making process, materials, workshop
- **Brand Heritage:** 2-3 historical or founder images for storytelling

## Key Design Principles

- **Premium Positioning:** Luxury aesthetic through typography, spacing, and imagery
- **Craftsmanship Focus:** Visual emphasis on handmade quality and materials
- **Clean Minimalism:** Generous whitespace, restrained color palette
- **Heritage Storytelling:** Traditional typography mixed with modern layouts
